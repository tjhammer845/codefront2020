Review Your DUO Account.

The devices you register with DUO will receive a security notification to authenticate your user session. These devices need to be available whenever you need access to a Yale resource (e.g. meetings, on campus, working remotely).

Recommend smartphone user setup includes:

  • Register at least two devices

  • Smartphone users setup this device as the primary

  • Secondary devices can include an office landline, tablet device, or an issued hard token

Recommend non-smartphone user setup includes:

  • Register at least two devices

  • Primary and secondary devices can include an office landline, tablet device, or an issued hard token
